Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish. The Spaghetti is served coated in a rich, creamy sauce made from eggs, cheese, and cured pork. It comes with a savory, comforting flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500g spaghetti
  • 3l boiling water
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g diced pork
  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 250ml heavy cream
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 6 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp gochugaru
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil and cook spaghetti for 7-8 minutes
  2. Dice onions and garlic.
  3. On a second stove add olive oil to a heated pan. Add the diced pork belly to the pan. Top with onions and garlic, creating a small stack. Let it cook untouched until the pork belly develops a crispy crust on the bottom. Stir the pile and distribute the mixture evenly until everything is golden brown
  4. Once the mixture is golden brown, add heavy cream to the pan, boil for 1 minute and turn off the heat.
  5. Separate the yolk from the eggs, add the yolks to the sauce and stir evenly. Add salt, pepper and parmesan cheese to the sauce.
  6. Check the pasta and turn off the heat once it's done. Drain the pasta water but keep roughly one cup of pasta water for later.
  7. Add the pasta to the sauce and stir evenly. Add pasta water to the mixture.
  8. Plate the pasta and garnish with parsley, parmesan and gochugaru.

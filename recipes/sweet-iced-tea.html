Sweet Iced Tea

Description

A chilled tea beverage that's lightly sweetened with a smooth and refreshing taste. Perfect for warm weather.

Ingredients

  • 2 tea bags
  • 1l boiling water
  • 3 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Pour boiling water into a heat-proof pitcher and add tea bags.
  2. Cover and let it steep for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove tea bags and stir in honey until it is dissolved.
  4. Refrigerate for about 3 hours.

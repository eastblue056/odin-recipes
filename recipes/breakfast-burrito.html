Breakfast Burrito

Description

Warm flour tortillas filled with eggs, cheese, and meat of choice. This meal combines savory flavors in a convenient handheld wrap.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 onions
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 8 eggs
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 200g shredded cheese
  • 4 sheets aluminum foil

Steps

  1. Dice onions and garlic. Heat oil in a large pan and fry them until them until the onions are translucent.
  2. Place ground beef in the same pan and fry the mixture over medium-high heat until the meat is cooked evenly. Add salt, pepper and gochugaru for taste.
  3. Whisk eggs, salt. pepper and gochugaru. Heat oil in a large pan. Cook the egg mixture and occasionally over medium-high heat until the eggs are scrambled.
  4. Lay two tortillas on a cutting board so that they slightly overlap. Add the beef mixture, eggs and cheese to the center and roll them tightly into a burrito.
  5. Place the burrito seam-side down in a pan and cook for 1–2 minutes to seal it. Flip and cook for another 1–2 minutes.
  6. remove from the pan and wrap tightly in aluminum foil.
